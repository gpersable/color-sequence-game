How to Play

  1. Press any key to start
  2. Simon will show you which button you shall click on your next move, following the previous series of colors and tones
  3. Memorize the sequence
  4. Repeat the sequence by clicking on the buttons
  5. The series will progressively become longer and more complex as you level up

Example:

Sequences Simon You
First
GREEN		 GREEN GREEN
Second
GREEN → BLUE		 BLUE GREEN → BLUE
Third
GREEN → BLUE → RED		 RED GREEN → BLUE → RED
